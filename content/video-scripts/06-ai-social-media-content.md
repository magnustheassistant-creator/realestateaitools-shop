## Main Content (0:30-9:30)

### The Engagement Problem (0:30-1:30)
Let me tell you what I see agents do that kills their reach:

Post generic content about the market. "The market is hot!" That gets scrolled past.

Post listings all day. "Check out this beautiful 4 bedroom!" Nobody cares unless they're actively looking.

Post motivational quotes. "Success is a journey!" This is not why people follow a real estate agent.

What gets engagement is content that's useful, surprising, or makes people feel something — then wraps it in real estate context.

### The Content Framework (1:30-3:30)

#### Type 1: The Quick Win
Give them something actionable in 30 seconds.
"3 ways to boost your home value before selling" 
"Question to ask before hiring an agent"
"One fix that adds $5k to your offer"

People save these. Saving = algorithm sees value = shows to more people.

#### Type 2: The Inside Scoop
Nobody else has this information.
"Builder just dropped prices on these 5 neighborhoods"
"The exact street where homes are selling 10% over asking"
"New development that will change [AREA] forever"

This requires you to be in the market consistently. But when you see something, share it immediately.

#### Type 3: The Story
Humans are wired for narrative.
"How I helped a family find their dream home in 3 days"
"The offer that almost fell through — here's what saved it"
"Why I told my buyer to walk away (and she thanked me)"

#### Type 4: The Myth Bust
Challenge common beliefs.
"Everyone thinks you need 20% down. Here's what we actually see."
"The #1 mistake buyers make in this market"
"You've been asking the wrong question about pre-approval"

### The AI Content Creation System (3:30-6:30)

#### For Caption Writing:
"use this prompt for all captions:

"Write 5 social media captions for [PLATFORM — Instagram/TikTok/LinkedIn] about [TOPIC]. 

Requirements:
- Hook in the first line (stop the scroll)
- Value or entertainment in every line
- End with a question or call-to-action that invites comments
- Include relevant hashtags (mixed size — some big, some niche)
- Tone: conversational, expert, not salesy
- Length: [X] words for [PLATFORM]

Each caption should be distinct — different angle, different hook."

#### For Thread Creation:
"Create a Twitter/X thread about [TOPIC]. 

Structure:
- Hook tweet that stops the scroll
- 5-8 informative tweets
- Each tweet should stand alone (can be read without context)
- End with engagement question

Topic: [YOUR CONTENT IDEA]

Format each tweet on its own line, separated by ||||"

#### For Video Scripting:
"Write a 60-second video script for TikTok/Reels about [TOPIC].

Structure:
- Hook (0-5 sec): Grab attention with surprising statement or question
- Body (5-50 sec): Teach something useful
- CTA (50-60 sec): Tell them what to do next, follow for more

Tone: Conversational, like you're talking to one person. Not scripted or stiff.

Topic: [DETAILED DESCRIPTION]

Include direction notes like [SHOW SCREEN] or [POV SHOT] in brackets."

### The Posting Calendar System (6:30-8:00)

Here's how I use AI to batch content creation:

#### Week 1: 5 Posts
- Sunday: 5 topics selected, prompts written
- Monday: Generate all captions using AI
- Tuesday: Record 3-5 video hooks
- Wednesday: Post and engage

#### Week 2: Engagement Focus  
- Use AI to generate reply templates for common comments
- Create story templates for real-time posting
- Generate 3-5 new posts based on engagement metrics

#### The Key Metric to Track
Not followers. Not likes. Comments per post.

Why? Comments = engagement signal to algorithm = more reach. Also, comments = conversation = relationship building = leads.

If a post gets high comments, do more of that type. If it flops, analyze why and adjust.

### Engagement Response Templates (8:00-9:30)

AI can help you respond to comments without sounding generic:

"Write 10 reply templates for a real estate agent on social media. 

Context: Post was about [TOPIC — home buying, listing, market, etc]
Comment type: [Questions, Compliments, Objections, Story sharing]

Requirements:
- Sound human, not auto-generated
- Add value with each response
- Move conversation forward
- Invite further engagement
- Don't be salesy in replies

Format as: If they say [COMMENT], respond with [YOUR REPLY]"

Keep these in a swipe file. When someone comments, you have a starting point — then personalize for that specific person.
