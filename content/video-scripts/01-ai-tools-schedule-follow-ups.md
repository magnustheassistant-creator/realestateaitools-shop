### Tool 1: Follow Up Boss (3:00-5:00)
Follow Up Boss isn't just a CRM — it's an automation machine. Here's exactly how I use their AI features.

First, every new lead gets automatically tagged and entered into a sequence. I have a set of 12 different sequences depending on lead source — FSBO, expired, sphere, Zillow, etc.

Then the AI analyzes each lead's behavior. When someone visits your website, it logs it. When they open an email, it knows. When they click a link, it scores them. High engagement score means they go to the top of your priority list automatically.

The automation rules are where the magic happens. You can set triggers like: if lead hasn't responded in 48 hours, send this text. If they still haven't responded, send this email. If they engage with a specific property, trigger this sequence.

The cost is $59/month and it's worth every penny. I've cut my admin time by 60% since implementing this.

### Tool 2: ChatGPT for Personalized Outreach (5:00-7:00)
Now here's the part most agents get wrong. They use templates that sound like templates. "Hi [NAME], I hope this finds you well." That's not personal — and leads can tell.

Instead, I use ChatGPT to create genuinely personalized first contact. Here's my exact prompt structure:

"I am a real estate agent in [CITY] working with buyers in the [PRICE RANGE] market. Write a 3-sentence introduction to a lead named [NAME] who [SPECIFIC DETAIL FROM THEIR PROFILE OR BEHAVIOR]. Focus on [THEIR APPARENT GOALS]. Keep it conversational, not salesy."

This produces messages that actually sound human. And the key is personalization based on what you know about them — their budget, their timeline, what properties they've viewed.

### Tool 3: Calendar.com for Scheduling (7:00-8:30)
The final piece is eliminating scheduling friction. When a lead wants to meet, you don't want 10 emails back and forth to find a time.

Calendar.com connects to your existing calendar and lets leads book based on your real availability. But the AI part is the smart reminders — it sends reschedule reminders 24 hours in advance, follows up if a meeting gets cancelled, and fills gaps in your calendar automatically with targeted outreach.

Each message is partially personalized using ChatGPT, then automated through Follow Up Boss. I spend 20 minutes a week managing this entire pipeline.
