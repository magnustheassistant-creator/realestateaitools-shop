Last updated: May 16, 2026

AI Tools for Real Estate ("we," "our," or "us") operates the website https://realestateaitools.shop. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Email address — if you subscribe to our newsletter (no spam, unsubscribe anytime)
  • Comments and feedback — any content you voluntarily submit via our contact forms
  • Affiliate link clicks — we may track clicks on affiliate links for attribution purposes

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

We use standard analytics tools that may collect:

  • Browser type and version
  • Device type (mobile/desktop)
  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • Referring website or source

This data is aggregated and anonymous — we cannot identify you personally from it.

1.3 Cookies

We use essential cookies for website functionality and optional analytics cookies. You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings. Our full cookie practices are described below.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To send newsletters you've subscribed to (you can unsubscribe at any time)
  • To improve our website and content based on aggregate analytics
  • To respond to your inquiries and feedback
  • To display relevant affiliate and advertising content
  • To comply with legal obligations

3. Affiliate Disclosure

AI Tools for Real Estate is a participant in various affiliate programs, including the Amazon Associates Program. This means we may earn commissions from qualifying purchases made through affiliate links on this site at no additional cost to you.

Affiliate links are clearly marked. We only recommend tools we believe provide genuine value to real estate professionals. Affiliate relationships do not influence our editorial content or recommendations.

4. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services that collect data independently:

  • Google Analytics — for website traffic analytics (you can opt out via Google's opt-out browser add-on)
  • Newsletter service — email address storage for newsletter delivery (compliant with CAN-SPAM and GDPR)
  • Amazon Associates — affiliate program operated by Amazon

5. Data Retention

We retain newsletter subscriber data until you unsubscribe. Analytics data is retained in aggregate form for up to 26 months. Any personally identifiable feedback is retained for up to 12 months or until the inquiry is resolved, whichever is longer.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Opt out of marketing emails at any time (click "unsubscribe" in any email)
  • Object to certain processing activities

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email below.

7. Data Security

We implement standard security measures including HTTPS encryption, secure hosting, and access controls. No method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed to individuals under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy regularly.
