What to Look for in an AI CRM for Real Estate

Before comparing options, here's what separates a real estate CRM from a generic one — and what the best AI features actually do for your business:

Lead Capture & Auto-Response

The fastest leads close fastest. The best AI CRMs capture leads from your website, Zillow, Realtor.com, and social media automatically — then fire off a personalized response within 60 seconds. If you're manually replying to leads hours later, you're already behind.

AI Lead Scoring

Not all leads are equal. AI lead scoring analyzes hundreds of data points — how they found you, browsing behavior, engagement with previous emails, property type interest — and assigns a hot-to-cold score. Focus your time on the leads most likely to close.

Automated Follow-Up Sequences

The average lead requires 8-12 touches before they convert. AI CRMs build and execute multi-week follow-up sequences automatically — texts, emails, voicemails — so no lead goes cold because you got busy showing a house.

Pipeline & Deal Tracking

Visual pipelines let you see exactly where every lead and deal stands. AI surfaces deals that have gone stale and prompts you to take action before opportunities die.

Text & Call Automation

The best AI CRMs for real estate agents integrate SMS and ringless voicemails directly, letting you run full outreach campaigns without switching between five different apps.

Team Collaboration

If you're running a team, look for shared inboxes, agent-to-agent task assignment, and commission tracking. AI can route leads to the right agent based on territory, availability, or specialization.
