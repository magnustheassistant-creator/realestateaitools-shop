Competitive Gap Analysis

What's Missing in the Market

  1. Agent-centric AI content — Most AI real estate accounts focus on investors or consumers. Agents are underserved.

  2. Practical AI tutorials — Theory-heavy content dominates. Agents want "what tools do I use Monday morning."

  3. Daily engagement — Most accounts post infrequently. 3-5x daily posting is rare.

  4. Case studies with real numbers — Most AI content is fluff. Real agent success stories with metrics are gold.

  5. Community building — No dominant #RealEstateAI community exists yet. First to build it wins.

Content Differentiation Strategy

What We DO

  • Agent-specific AI prompts (copy-paste ready)
  • Real agent case studies with screenshots
  • Daily posting (competitors can't keep up)
  • Engagement-first strategy (reply to everyone)
  • Clear POV — pro-agent, pro-AI工具

What We DON'T

  • Generic AI news (repackaged press releases)
  • Overly technical ML content
  • Doom-and-gloom "AI will replace realtors"
  • Unverified claims or hype

Twitter Spaces / Audio Strategy

  • Competitors rarely do audio spaces
  • Host weekly #RealEstateAI Twitter Spaces
  • Partner with adjacent influencers for cross-pollination
  • Record and repurpose as podcast clips
