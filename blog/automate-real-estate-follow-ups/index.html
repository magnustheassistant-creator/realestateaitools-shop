How to Set Up a Follow-Up Automation Sequence (Step by Step)

Setting up AI follow-ups isn't complicated, but it requires structure. Here's a proven 7-day follow-up sequence you can implement with any of the tools above.

Day 1 — Instant Response (AI)

Trigger: New lead inquiry arrives.

Action: AI sends a personalized response within 60 seconds. If it's an email inquiry, AI replies referencing the specific property they asked about and offers to schedule a call. If it's a text, AI responds with a friendly confirmation and asks a qualifying question.

Example text: "Hi [Name]! Thanks for reaching out about [Property Address]. I'd love to chat — are you available for a quick 5-minute call today? I've got times at 2pm and 5pm if either works!"

Day 2 — Follow-Up Email (AI)

Trigger: No response to Day 1.

Action: AI sends a follow-up email with new value — market data for their area, a relevant listing, or a helpful guide. Keep it short and useful.

Example subject line: "Quick question about your home search — [City]"

Day 3 — Phone Call + Voicemail (AI)

Trigger: No response to emails.

Action: AI places a call to the lead's number. If no answer, it leaves a personalized voicemail. If the lead answers, AI has a conversation, qualifies them, and can schedule an appointment directly.

Day 5 — Text Message (AI)

Trigger: Still no response.

Action: AI sends a text message. Keep it casual and low-pressure. The goal is to stay top of mind without being annoying.

Example text: "Hey [Name] — just circling back on the listing at [Address]. Still very much available if you're still interested. No pressure either way — happy to send you comparable properties if helpful!"

Day 7 — Market Update Email (AI)

Trigger: Lead still hasn't booked a call.

Action: AI sends a market update relevant to their search criteria — new listings, price reductions, or market trends. This provides genuine value and reopens the conversation naturally.

Day 14 — Re-engagement (AI)

Trigger: Lead went cold.

Action: AI sends a "checking in" message with a reason to reconnect — a new listing that just hit the market, a sold comparably, or an invitation to an open house.

Day 30 — Past Client / Sphere Re-Engagement

Trigger: Lead went cold but has potential as a future transaction.

Action: AI adds them to a 30/60/90-day nurture sequence for sphere re-engagement. Quarterly check-ins, holiday messages, market reports — all automated.

Pro Tips for AI Follow-Up Success

1. Personalize everything. The #1 reason follow-up messages feel spammy is generic copy. Use the lead's name, reference the property they inquired about, and mention something specific to their situation. AI tools make this easy with merge tokens and behavioral triggers.

2. Don't over-automate human moments. Once a lead is actively negotiating or in contract, step in personally. AI is a supplement to your relationships, not a replacement for them on high-stakes interactions.

3. Test your timing. The best follow-up windows for real estate are Tuesday–Thursday, 10am–12pm and 3pm–5pm local time. Most tools let you schedule sends by timezone — use that feature.

4. Keep compliance in mind. Texting and calling automation has legal requirements — especially around TCPA and do-not-contact lists. Choose tools that handle consent tracking and unsubscribe management automatically.

5. Refresh your sequences quarterly. What worked in January might not resonate in May. Review your open rates, response rates, and conversion rates every quarter and update your copy accordingly.
