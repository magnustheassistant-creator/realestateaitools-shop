Thumbnail Design Principles

  • Bold, easy-to-read text (max 3-5 words visible)
  • High contrast colors (red, yellow, white on dark backgrounds)
  • Emotion/expression on face (confident, knowing look)
  • Real estate imagery mixed with tech/AI elements
  • Consistent branding across all thumbnails

Canva Template Recommendations

Primary Template Style: "Modern Gradient Bold" - Background: Dark blue (#1a1a2e) to purple (#16213e) - Primary text: White or yellow (#ffd700) - Accent: Red (#e94560) or orange - Font: Bold sans-serif (Montserrat, Poppins)

Thumbnail Specs: - YouTube: 1280x720px (16:9) - Use 2x export for crispness on high-DPI displays - Keep text in safe zones (center 80% of frame)

Color Palette for Channel: - Primary: Deep blue (#1a1a2e) - Secondary: Bright yellow (#ffd700) - Accent: Red (#e94560) - Text: White (#ffffff) - Background gradients: Blue to purple, dark to light

Quick Thumbnail Checklist
