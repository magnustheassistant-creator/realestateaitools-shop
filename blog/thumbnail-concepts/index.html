Typography

  • **Headings:** Montserrat Bold (Google Fonts) — clean, modern, professional
  • **Subtext:** Inter Medium — highly readable at small sizes
  • **Numbers/Badges:** Impact or Bebas Neue — maximum impact

Thumbnail Dimensions

  • **Standard:** 1280 x 720 px (16:9)
  • **Safe Zone:** Center 1080 x 620 px (avoid text/icons at edges)
  • **Text Max:** Headline 3-5 words, Subtext 3-6 words

Canva Template Recommendations

For All Thumbnails: Create Brand Kit

  1. Go to Canva > Brand Kit
  2. Add colors: #1E3A5F, #FF6B35, #FFB800, #2D2D2D
  3. Add fonts: Montserrat Bold, Inter Medium
  4. Upload Magnus logo (when available)
  5. Save as "Magnus RE AI Thumbnails" template set

Thumbnail Consistency Rules

  1. **Always Include:**
  2. Magnus face/brand (small, bottom-right corner)
  3. Bold keyword-driven headline (3-5 words)
  4. 1 supporting detail (2-6 words)
  1. **Never Include:**
  2. More than 3 font sizes
  3. More than 3 colors
  4. Blurry/low-quality images
  5. More than 1 human face
  1. **Font Hierarchy:**
  2. Main headline: Largest, boldest, white or orange
  3. Supporting detail: Medium, white or gold
  4. Magnus credit: Smallest, white, bottom corner
