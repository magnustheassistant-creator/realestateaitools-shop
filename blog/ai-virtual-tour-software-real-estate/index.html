Best AI Virtual Tour Software for Real Estate

1. Matterport

Price: $13/month (Starter) | $69/month (Pro+)

Best for: Full 3D dollhouse tours with no camera restrictions

Matterport remains the industry leader for AI-powered 3D tours. Their scanning app turns any smartphone into a 3D capture device — no special camera required. You walk through a property tapping your phone at each scan point, and the AI assembles a complete 3D model with dollhouse views, room measurements, and floor plans.

The AI also auto-detects doors, windows, and stairs to create accurate floor layouts. For listing presentations, the Matterport embed works on any website and tracks viewer engagement. Many top agents use it as their primary differentiator — a property that buyers can explore remotely gets more showings, more offers, and faster closings.

Key features:

  • AI-powered 3D model assembly from smartphone scans
  • Auto floor plan generation
  • Measurement tools accurate to 99%
  • Embeddable player for any website
  • Viewer's eye-tracking analytics

3. TourWizard

Price: From $39/month

Best for: Quick, no-frills virtual tours with fast turnaround

TourWizard is a straightforward 360° tour platform that emphasizes simplicity. You upload photos, the AI assembles them into a navigable tour, and you get an embeddable link. It's less sophisticated than Matterport but costs significantly less and handles the core use case well.

New for 2025: TourWizard's AI noise reduction for twilight photos and its auto-enhancement filter that makes listing photos look professionally edited. Agents using it for same-day listings appreciate the 15-minute turnaround on tours.

Key features:

  • 15-minute tour turnaround
  • Auto photo enhancement AI
  • Twilight noise reduction
  • Unlimited tours on paid plans
  • Simple embed codes

5. Real Virtual Tours by iguide

Price: Bundled with IGuide hardware/service

Best for: High-volume agents wanting a dedicated hardware + software solution

iGuide's dedicated camera system ($1,200+) combines a 360° camera with a tablet and AI-powered stitching software. It produces Matterport-quality tours but with a more streamlined workflow designed for agents who do multiple tours per week.

The AI handles room detection, floor plan generation, and measurement — the same as Matterport but with dedicated hardware that speeds up the scanning process. If you're doing more than 10 tours per month, the investment pays off in quality and consistency.

Key features:

  • Integrated 360° camera hardware
  • AI room mapping and floor plans
  • MLS-ready output
  • Measurement accuracy to 1%
  • Dedicated support

Frequently Asked Questions

Do buyers actually use virtual tours?

Yes — and they prefer them. Properties with 3D tours receive 50% more inquiries than those without, and buyers who use tours are more likely to schedule an in-person showing because they feel more confident about the property.

Do I need special equipment?

No. Modern smartphones work well for Matterport and CloudPano tours. Dedicated 360° cameras ($300-$600) improve quality but aren't required to get started.

Are AI virtual tours worth the monthly subscription cost?

For agents doing more than 2 listings per month, yes. A virtual tour differentiates your listing, generates more leads, and helps properties sell faster — easily justifying a $13-$69/month subscription.
