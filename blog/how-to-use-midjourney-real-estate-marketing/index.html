What Is Midjourney?

Midjourney is an AI image generation platform that runs through Discord. You enter a text description (called a "prompt"), and the AI generates images matching that description in various styles—photorealistic, illustrated, artistic, or architectural. The quality of outputs rivals professional photography and graphic design.

Midjourney works on a subscription model, with a free tier that gives you limited generations. For serious real estate marketing use, the Standard Plan ($30/month) provides unlimited generations and commercial usage rights, which is essential for listing and advertising use.

Key advantages for real estate agents: - Create virtual staging for empty or furnished rooms - Generate lifestyle imagery showing target demographics - Design unique neighborhood and community visuals - Produce social media content at scale - Create illustrated property ads and flyers - Generate architectural concepts for off-market properties

How to Create Virtual Staging with Midjourney

Virtual staging is one of the highest-value uses of Midjourney for real estate. Instead of paying $200-500 per room for professional virtual staging, you can generate unlimited staging concepts at a fraction of the cost.

The Process

  1. Get good photos of the empty room — Use your phone in landscape mode, good lighting, from each corner and the doorway
  2. Upload the photo to Discord — Use the paperclip icon to attach
  3. Use the /describe command — This generates prompt suggestions based on your room photo
  4. Refine the prompt — Add your staging preferences (modern, coastal, industrial, etc.)
  5. Generate multiple options — Create 4 variations to choose from
  6. Upscale the best option — Use the U1-U4 buttons to upscale your favorite
  7. Edit if needed — Use Photoshop or Canva to blend with the actual room

Important Tip: --no furniture vs --cref

When staging, you may want to use the --cref (character reference) or --sref (style reference) parameters to maintain consistency, but for pure virtual staging, starting from scratch with a well-written prompt gives better results.

Virtual Staging Best Practices

  • Be realistic — Don't stage with furniture that wouldn't fit the room
  • Match the target buyer — Young family? Stage with family-friendly furniture. Luxury buyer? Stage with high-end designer pieces
  • Use consistent lighting — All rooms in a listing should feel like they were shot at the same time of day
  • Clearly disclose AI staging — Most MLS boards require disclosure when photos are digitally altered
  • Blend carefully — Use Photoshop's masking tools to blend AI furniture realistically into the real room

Ethical Guidelines: When to Disclose AI-Generated Images

Honesty in real estate advertising is not just ethical—it's legally required in most states. Here are the guidelines:

When You MUST Disclose AI-Generated Content

  • Virtual staging — Any digitally furnished or renovated room photo must be labeled as "Virtual Staging" or "Digital Enhancement" in the MLS and in advertisements
  • Imagined/future views — If you show what a view could look like or a renovation concept, it must be labeled as "Artist's Conception" or "Digital Rendering"
  • Social media — FTC guidelines require clear disclosure when AI-generated imagery is used in advertising

When AI-Generated Images Are Appropriate

  • Blog posts and educational content
  • Social media posts (with disclosure)
  • Email marketing (with disclosure)
  • Printed materials where the AI nature is clearly communicated

When to Avoid AI-Generated Images

  • Any representation of actual property condition
  • False impressions of views, lighting, or space
  • As a substitute for honest listing photography

How to Edit and Polish Midjourney Outputs

Midjourney generates excellent images, but you'll almost always want to refine them:

Tools for Refinement

  1. Canva — Add text overlays, adjust colors, crop for social media
  2. Photoshop — Remove unwanted elements, blend multiple images, correct colors
  3. ** Lightroom** — Color correction and grading to match your brand
  4. Remove.bg — Instantly remove backgrounds for transparent PNGs
  5. Topaz Gigapixel — AI-powered upscaling for print-quality enlargement

Common Corrections to Make

  • Adjust color temperature to match other listing photos
  • Remove any distorted or unrealistic elements
  • Scale furniture to realistic proportions
  • Blend edges where AI meets real photography
  • Add your branding/logo for consistency
